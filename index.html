Who are we?

This space programme was launched by a group of selfless individuals on a Thursday night in the heart of Manchester. The aim was quite simple, let’s get something into space and join the race of other people trying to do the same around the world.

What have we done so far?

For an overview of the upcoming and previous missions take a look at our missions page.

But it’s a programme, right? So more than one launch?

Yup. So having the word programme was very important. The idea was not just have one launch and then just go home. This is to be a series of hopefully many launches, which should capture not only enthusiasts but the general public as well. we are not restricting ourselves to a particular technology or methodology. This is going to a programme for Manchester as whole.

Where and when do you meet?

We have two types of meetings: Build Meetings and General Meetings.
